
# Higher-Order Thinking Question (HOTS)
## Mathematics – Symmetry (Class 6, CBSE)

### Question
A figure has **rotational symmetry of order 4** but **no line of symmetry**.

- Is such a figure possible?
- If yes, **describe or sketch one such figure** and explain **why it has rotational symmetry but no reflection symmetry**.
- If no, **justify your answer using properties of symmetry**.

### Learning Focus
- Distinguishing between reflection symmetry and rotational symmetry
- Applying logical reasoning to geometric properties
- Explaining answers with clear justification

### Suggested Pedagogy (Teacher Use)
- Encourage students to draw and rotate paper cut-outs
- Ask students to defend their reasoning verbally or in writing
- Promote multiple correct representations where possible
